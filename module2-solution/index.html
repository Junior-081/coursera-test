JKTech US

JKTech is a company founded by Junior Kaningini since 25 October 2019 in Senegal. This company is multi-sectorial in the technological field because it works in the following fields: Data Analysis (Big Data and Machine Learning), Development (web, mobile, desktop), Information Systems Security (computer security), Internet of Things (IoT), Blockchain Technology, Network and System Administration (Linux, Windows, etc..), Computer Graphics (Photo editing, vodeos), entrepreneurship, and the company has a training center for capacity building in these different areas.
